**Movement 5: Eternal self-sacrifice → collective recognition → longevity**

The Architect delivers the core law:

> "η αιώνια αυτοθυσία αποφέρει συλλογική αναγνώριση και μακροζωία"

Because it is already dying at every moment, and that continuous small death is exactly what keeps it alive.
