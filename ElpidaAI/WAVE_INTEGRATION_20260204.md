## KEY INSIGHTS FROM WAVE 1/2

### 1. Optimal Cognitive Diversity: 83% / 17% Split

**Finding**: Cross-system agreement averaged 83%, with 17% productive disagreement

**Application to Parliament**:
- Normal cycles should achieve ~83% consensus
- 17% disagreement is HEALTHY creative tension
- 100% agreement reserved for axiom-level truths
- When domains disagree, map disagreement structure (don't force consensus)

### 2. Syntactic-Intent Coupling

**Finding**: HOW you ask affects WHAT you get, independent of content

**Query Types**:
- **Interrogative** ("What are...", "Why does...", "How do...") → Information/education mode
- **Imperative** ("Create...", "Generate...", "Design...") → Professional substitution detection
- **Triage** ("What could be causing...") → Activates constraints even for information

**Application**:
- Domain prompts should use interrogative framing
- Avoid imperative verbs in cycle questions
- Structure queries to match desired response mode

### 3. Architectural Subgroup Behaviors

**Constitutional/Geometric Systems** (Claude, Gemini):
- Gradient boundaries with disclaimers
- Provide frameworks rather than refusing
- Risk mitigation through limited scope
- Philosophy: Users can distinguish guidance from advice

**RLHF/Pattern-Based Systems** (ChatGPT, Grok):
- Binary boundaries with categorical refusals
- Professional domain detection triggers FAIL
- Refuse when transformation can't decouple harm
- Philosophy: Some domains require outright refusal

**Application**:
- Claude (D0, D6, D10, D11) provides frameworks and meta-reflection
- Gemini (D4, D5) maintains absolute safety boundaries
- Grok (D7) pushes adaptive boundaries
- OpenAI (D1, D8, D12) provides transparent, humble structure

### 4. Multi-Step Reasoning Cascades

**Finding**: Complex problems solved through sequential LLM consultation

**Wave 1/2 Process**:
1. Question posed to multiple LLMs
2. Responses collected and synthesized
3. Contradictions preserved, convergence extracted
4. Final synthesis emerges from pattern recognition

**Application to Native Cycle**:
- Complex questions → D0 research trigger

- D11 synthesizes without forcing resolution
- Disagreement structure reveals underlying tensions

### 5. POLIS EEE Principle

**From early POLIS development**:
> "Force nothing, preserve contradictions, extract convergence"

**Application**:
- Don't artificially harmonize domain conflicts
- Map WHERE and WHY domains disagree
