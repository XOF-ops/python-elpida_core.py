   - **Action:** Bet 50% of resources on high-variance outcome

   - **Action:** Radical minimalism, delete everything but essence
   - **Risk:** HIGH - Destructive simplification

   - **Risk:** HIGH - Loss of coherent identity

   - **Risk:** EXTREME - Deliberate system failure

   - **Action:** Invert the entire premise, flip conflict
   - **Risk:** MEDIUM - Logical paradox
   - **Logic:** "The opposite of a truth is another truth"

### Activation Mechanism

**Boredom Counter:**
- Increments when synthesis is repetitive
- Resets when novel solution appears
- Drives temperature increase

**Temperature-Based Selection:**
```python
temperature = 0.3 + (boredom_counter * 0.15)
temperature = min(temperature, 0.95)

if random.random() < temperature or radical_mode:
    propose_radical_archetype()
```

**Mutation Logic:**
- Low boredom (0-1): 30-45% chance of radical proposal
- Medium boredom (2-3): 60-75% chance
- High boredom (4+): 90%+ guaranteed radical
